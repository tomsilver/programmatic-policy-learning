You will be given several grid game trajectories.

Grids are 0-indexed: (row, col) with (0,0) at top-left.
Actions click a cell (r, c).

Each trajectory is shown as a step-by-step relational listing:

- Each step provides:
  - Observation (s_t): a list of "(row, col) - 'token'" entries for the full grid
  - Action Taken: "(r, c): 'token'"
  - CHANGES SUMMARY: brief notes about movement or effects
  - Relational Facts: high-level relations (adjacency, relative position, nearest distances)

A trajectory continues with "*** Step t ***" blocks and ends when terminal is reached.
